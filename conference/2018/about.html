About

Motivation

-

The inaugral Performance Co-Pilot conference brings together a community of developers and performance analysts to discuss the latest developments in the PCP project. One day of deeply technical talks and tutorials will be provided by key developers of PCP (pcp.io), Vector (vectoross.io) and related technologies.
It provides a unique opportunity to meet with Red Hat, IBM, Netflix, Fujitsu and other community developers, collaborators and performance analysts from around the world. This event is free to attend, with limited seating capacity - please register early to guarantee your place! It is co-located with the Linux Foundation Open Source Summit (#ossummit) Japan.

Now in its 23rd year, PCP provides a framework and services to support system-level performance monitoring and management. It presents a unifying abstraction for all of the performance data in a system, and many tools for interrogating, retrieving and processing that data.
